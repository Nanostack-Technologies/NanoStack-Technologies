Timeline varies by complexity. A standard MVP takes 4-8 weeks, while enterprise-grade systems can range from 3-6 months. We prioritize agile, iterative delivery.
What technologies do you specialize in?
Our core stack includes Python/Django for backends, Next.js/React for frontends, and AI/Automation tools like OpenAI and n8n to streamline workflows.
Do you provide post-launch support?
Yes, we offer monthly maintenance packages covering security updates, performance monitoring, and new feature additions to ensure your software scales with you.
How do we get started?
Simply reach out via our contact form or WhatsApp. We'll schedule a discovery call to understand your needs and provide a detailed technical proposal.
